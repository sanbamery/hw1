Stanford: 4 on-campus beverages you should
be drinking

Victoria Kirst

Last updated Apr 7, 2017

1. Italian soda at Bytes

Bytes Cafe

When's the last time you've had an Italian soda? It had been a while for me, but I wanted to join my friend because she had been craving something sweet and refreshing for a few weeks. Bytes is a casual eatery that sells quick and easy food. They can whip up some great Italian sodas by mixing some flavored syrup and carbonated water. The Italian sodas at Bytes also have cream in them (or maybe they do if you ask, I honestly don't remember because my friend ordered this one for me). Overall, great tasting and refreshing, but it does leave a fizzy aftertaste.

Recommendation: Raspberry Italian Soda

2. tea at Pop Tea Bar

Pop Tea Bar

When your boba craving strikes, head over to Pop Tea Bar, a cute little bubble tea shop on Cambridge Ave, a short walk from campus (about 0.3 miles). They have some great drinks that are perfect for a warm spring day. Their tea is of high quality and the boba is soft, chewy, and sweet, which is essential. They have little bite-sized food options (like vegetable egg rolls and snack-size portions). They also have indoor seating, but they have a cute outdoor area in the back for warmer days.

Recommendation: Classic Milk Tea and Lattes - any flavor of popping boba!

3. at CoHo

Coupa Cafe

CoHo, a popular student hangout, has a particularly amazing Vanilla Bean Latte, only because CoHo is the only place I've seen that has it on campus (and I love my vanilla). I'm not exactly sure what a Vanilla Bean Latte is supposed to taste like. What I do know is the Vanilla Bean Latte I ordered on a whim from CoHo was delicious.

Recommendation: Vanilla Bean Latte

4. Agua Fresca at Nexus Cafe

If you're looking for an alternative to water and tea, Agua Fresca is something you should seek out. It's a soft, fresh and fruity beverage before you head to the check out line. Nexus Cafe has plenty of indoor and outdoor seating to enjoy the warm weather with a refreshing drink. The cafe is a little pricey, but it's great for a special occasion.

Recommendation: Agua Fresca
